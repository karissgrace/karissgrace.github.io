Word lover

Powerful.  Connecting.  Important.
I have loved words for as long as I can remember.  Words have always come as a natural way of expressing myself. Whether writing, reading, or speaking, words are a connecting point that can be life-giving. I strive to use this tool to create a better for every life I touch. 

    world traveler

    Traveling has become one of my very favorite parts of life.  Whether traveling to national parks in the United States or taking a trip overseas, I love the thrill of exploring a new place.  I have visited 6 different countries and islands overseas, but my destination list is ever growing. 

      music maker

      Music has been an integral part of my life ever since I can remember.  Listening to music in the car and picking out a tune on the church piano are some of my earliest memories of music.  I have since taken 11 years of piano lessons and taught myself to play the ukulele and mountain dulcimer.  Music is a vital piece of my existence. 

        Truth proclaimer

        Growing up in a Christian home, it was easy for me to take for granted the faith that I have grown up surrounded by.  It was not until I got into high school and even college that I began to realize just how important making my faith my own is.  I am called to be a proclaimer of truth: that I am a blood-bought sinner, redeemed by God and chosen to become a part of his holy race.  His grace is sufficient for me, and his words are true.  I am an instrument of his grace.
